About Lothego

Lothego is an innovative EdTech startup in Nigeria that harnesses research-informed, context-relevant, bite-size engaging content to foster digital literacy through its dedicated mobile app, packed with various courses on key digital topics. By equipping users with this essential knowledge and skills, Lothego plays a pivotal role in ushering more individuals into the digital economy and bridging the digital divide.

Through our unique "plan-do-reflect" model, learners can go beyond understanding digital concepts and actively practice and internalise them, paving the way for genuine digital empowerment.
